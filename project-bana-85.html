Overview

700+ acres, engineered as one system.

Bana 85 is a 700-plus-acre industrial development for Rooker, with up to 6–7 million square feet of potential building area.

At this scale, small inefficiencies multiply into enormous cost — major topography, rock-excavation risk, roadway elevations affecting dozens of lots, and stream and wetland impacts. By running survey, planning, and engineering as one workflow — LiDAR and drone capture, SiteOps grading models, earthwork balancing, and Army Corps permitting — GCI maximized developable acreage, cut earthwork and rock-related cost, and delivered faster through in-house survey and engineering.
