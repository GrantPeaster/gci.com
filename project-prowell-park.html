Prowell Park

Land PlanningLandscape ArchitectureCivil Engineering
Overview

Refining a master plan around the owner's vision.

Prowell Park is a ~300-acre public recreation park between two residential subdivisions, with high public visibility.

GCI inherited a master plan and made it buildable — re-evaluating grading and drainage against the actual topography, adjusting fields, parking, and circulation for neighborhood compatibility (noise, lighting, buffers), and phasing the infrastructure for future growth. The result was a stronger, more buildable plan with better public compatibility and a lasting community asset.
