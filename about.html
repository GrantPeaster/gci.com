Who we are

Built on better planning.

At GCI, we believe successful projects are built on better planning. Most firms get involved after the critical decisions are made — our approach begins earlier.

By integrating land planning, surveying, civil engineering, and landscape architecture from the very beginning, we help owners, developers, municipalities, and institutions understand what a site can realistically support — identifying constraints before they become costly delays, and making the decisions that matter most while they still carry the greatest impact.

Because successful projects aren't built on assumptions. They're built on better planning.
