High-density site data, captured from the air and ground.

LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) captures millions of measured points to build a precise 3D model of a site's surface.

For large or hard-to-reach properties, it dramatically reduces field time while increasing detail — giving our planners and engineers a rich, accurate basemap from the very start. Because survey, planning, and engineering sit together at GCI, that data never gets lost in a handoff between firms.
