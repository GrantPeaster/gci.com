Camp Buckhead

Land SurveyingLand PlanningCivil EngineeringConstruction Admin
Overview

A high-end subdivision on a severely constrained site.

Camp Buckhead is a 291-acre conservation subdivision for Thrive More Advisors — 31 lake lots plus estate lots up to 15 acres.

The site had extremely poor soils that ruled out individual septic, plus no public sewer or water and high aesthetic expectations. GCI located the single viable area for a community septic system (coordinated with a specialty firm), tied the development into Piedmont Water's nearby system, and held the design standard with weekly construction administration — earning successful permitting and a cohesive, high-end community.
