Overview

Infrastructure planned for today and tomorrow.

Morgan County Park is a ~70-acre public recreation complex — planned and laid out by GCI from the ground up.

GCI led the full site planning, recreation layout, grading, drainage, utilities, and survey integration, then designed a flexible, expandable system so each phase builds on the last instead of replacing it. Construction layout and field support from the same team produced a highly functional facility and a smoother build.
