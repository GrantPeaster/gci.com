Putting the design back on the ground.

Construction staking translates the approved design into physical markers on site, so contractors build exactly to plan.

Accurate, timely staking keeps crews on line and on grade, prevents costly rework, and keeps the schedule moving. Because we surveyed and helped engineer the site, our staking ties seamlessly back to the original design intent.
